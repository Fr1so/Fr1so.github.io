Getting Started

1. Enable Sound

Ensure your phone's volume is turned up to experience the full SoundMap journey.

2. Enable Location

Allow location access to discover nearby sounds as you explore.

For iOS Users

  • Open Settings -> Apps
  • Scroll to Safari
  • Enable "Location Services"

For Android Users

  • Open Chrome settings
  • Go to Site settings
  • Allow "Location" access
